Origin Story

We first started our restaurant from where I live which is Wausau, Wi. It was tough at first as nobody did not wanted to come to rented building that looked not interesting at all. However, soon after later, people started to realize how good our food was, then many people started to post the stories about us in their own social media. Thankfully, we were able to spread our table-in-restaurant to Stevens Point, and even to Madison!
